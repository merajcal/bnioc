BNIOC Cricket Academy Rules & Regulations

Important guidelines for all academy participants

Attendance & Timing

  • Students must arrive 15 minutes before the session starts
  • Regular attendance is mandatory for all enrolled programs
  • Inform the academy 24 hours in advance for any absence
  • Late arrivals may not be permitted to join the session

Dress Code & Equipment

  • Proper cricket whites or academy uniform is mandatory
  • Students must bring their own protective gear
  • Non-marking cricket shoes are compulsory
  • Academy will provide bats and balls for training

Conduct & Behavior

  • Respectful behavior towards coaches and fellow students
  • No use of mobile phones during training sessions
  • Follow all safety instructions given by coaches
  • Maintain discipline and sportsmanship at all times

Fees & Payment

  • Fees must be paid in advance before the program starts
  • No refund policy for voluntary withdrawals
  • Additional charges for tournaments and competitions
  • Late payment may result in suspension from training

Safety & Health

  • Medical certificate required for all students
  • Inform coaches about any injuries or health issues
  • Academy not responsible for personal belongings
  • Insurance coverage recommended for all participants

Disciplinary Actions

  • Warning system for rule violations
  • Suspension for repeated misconduct
  • Termination for serious violations without refund
  • Academy reserves the right to dismiss any student

Note: All students and parents must read and agree to these rules before enrollment. The academy reserves the right to modify these rules as needed.

For any clarifications, contact us at +91 79740 94110 or info@batkhelo.com
